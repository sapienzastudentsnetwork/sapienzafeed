Department of Computer Science (DCS)

Danilo Avola is the Head and Co-Founder of the Prometheus Laboratory.

The Prometheus Laboratory is the Faculty of Information Engineering, Informatics, and Statistics reference point in terms of Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Machine and Deep Learning, Robotics, and Dronistics. Our Projects, Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree cover all the Departments of the Faculty, i.e., the Department of Computer Science, the Department of Computer, Control and Management Engineering "Antonio Ruberti", the Department of Information Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications, and the Department of Statistical Sciences.
