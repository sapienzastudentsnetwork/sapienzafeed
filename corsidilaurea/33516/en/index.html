07/03/2026 - 1st Extraordinary Exam Session

The requests you submitted through the form to participate in the 1st extraordinary Cybersecurity session have been shared with all professors who can open exam sessions in the Infostud system and hold exams from March 16 to April 20.

24/02/2026 - Notice for graduating students of your master degree program

The deadline for uploading the thesis and sending the PDF to the academic office remains March 13, by 11:59 PM.

Afterwards, the approval of thesis supervisors in the Infostud system will take place, which is necessary to organize the sessions with the students who will actually be presenting.

The defense session will be scheduled starting March 27. No end-of-session period has been intentionally set.

Once all requests are approved, we will inform you if it will be possible to schedule the session before the Easter break.
