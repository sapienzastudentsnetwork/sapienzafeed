Emanuele Panizzi is Associate Professor in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy. He runs GamificationLab, a group dedicated to Context-Aware Interaction on mobile devices, Human-Computer Interaction, Gamification, and app design.
His current research mainly focuses on applying machine learning to detect users' behavior and the context in the two domains of smart-parking and earthquake detection. This research's experimental part consists of designing mobile user interfaces with implicit interaction and crowdsensing applications.
He co-authored more than 70 publications. He teaches HCI and software architecture courses. He has been a consultant for major national and international corporations.
