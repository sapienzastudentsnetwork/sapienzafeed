Su richiesta

Daniele De Sensi is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome. He was previously an ETH Postdoctoral Fellow at ETH Zurich (2020-2022) and held PostDoc and Ph.D. positions at the University of Pisa, where he earned his Ph.D. . He has co-authored more than 40 peer-reviewed papers on High-Performance Computing (HPC). His research spans interconnection networks design, optimization of parallel applications, and energy-aware computing. He has served on the program committees of several top-tier HPC conferences, including SC IPDPS, ICS, and HPDC.
