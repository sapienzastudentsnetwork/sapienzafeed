The degree course is delivered predominantly via distance learning.

Read the FAQ for better orientation:

  • Telelearning FAQ
  • To access the course you need to have access credentials to the platform
  • How do I get the course access credentials and enter the Unitelma platform?

The credentials to access the video lessons and teaching material on the Unitelma website will be sent to students within 7 working days of paying the first installment of the registration fee.

The credentials will be sent to the email address specified when registering.

In case of failure to receive the credentials within the deadline indicated above, students can send a communication to the following address: ivana.crispino@unitelmasapienza.it, putting in cc. the prof. Stefano Faralli faralli@di.uniroma1.it

For each course included in the teaching load, students will benefit from:

  • audio-video recordings of lessons;
  • online tutors;
  • interaction via the e-learning platform;
  • teaching material specially developed to support distance learning.

Every year there will also be some synchronous activities, for example web seminars.

The exams are held in person only at the Sapienza campus in Rome.
