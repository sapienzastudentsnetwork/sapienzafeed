Research interests
Calculus of Variations, Geometric Measure Theory, Applied Mathematics, Materials Science.
My research focuses on variational models in elasticity, in particular on fracture mechanics, discrete dislocations in crystals, topological singularities and phase transitions. I also deal with variational models in image segmentation and geometric flows.
