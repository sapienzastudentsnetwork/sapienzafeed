I am Associate Professor at the Department of Information Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications (DIET) of Sapienza University of Rome, Italy. I received her Ph.D.in Electronics Engineering from the University of Roma Tre. I have participated in the MPEG-4 standardization activity within the ISO MPEG-4 Core Experiment on Automatic Video Segmentation. I am co-author of more than a hundred journal and conference papers, two book chapters and several ISO MPEG-4 Contributing Documents. My research interests range from statistical signal processing, image deconvolution and restoration, biomedical signal processing, to video encoding, processing and networking. I have served in several conferences as Technical Program Co-chair, Steering Board member, Publicity Chair, Session Chair, Technical Program Committee member. I have served/I am serving in the Editorial Boards of different international journals on the topics of Signals and Multimedia Processing. I am currently an IEEE Senior Member.
