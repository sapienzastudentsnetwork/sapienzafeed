Information, Student affairs offices, support

You are never alone at Sapienza! There is an office for every need you may have.

  • For general information you can turn to the HELLO Office ↗;
  • For administrative procedures contact the relevant Student Affairs Offices ↗; All administrative procedures (fees, part-time, transfers, etc.) are detailed in the Student Regulations. ↗
  • For academic issues (lesson time, schedule, exams, etc.) go to the SORT Office ↗ or use the on-line Infostud Platform;
  • For technical support click on Infostud Platform ↗
  • For students with international degrees ↗

Moreover, if you have any further issues, you can turn to the Student Ombudsperson, faculty ombudsperson and student representative. Click here ↗

Would you like to visit Sapienza’s beautiful museums?

The skeleton of a sperm whale, 2 Neanderthals found in Rome, 1,200 plaster casts, 1 million plants at the Herbarium, 34,000 minerals, 1,000 fossils, a dispensary, the dish of the first pharaoh of the II Dynasty, the spectrograph used by Enrico Fermi, 750 boxes of lepidopterans…you may find all this wealth in the 18 Sapienza museums.
The Sapienza Museum Network also includes the extraordinary Botanical Garden and a contemporary art laboratory.
