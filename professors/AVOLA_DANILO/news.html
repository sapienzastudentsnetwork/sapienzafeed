« News for AVOLA DANILO

Danilo Avola is a Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome. His research spans Advanced Artificial Intelligence applied to Computer Vision, Scene Understanding, Medical Imaging, Human-Computer Interaction, Human Behaviour Analysis, Biometric Systems, Multimodal Interaction, and XR (Virtual and Augmented Reality). He also develops AI-driven approaches for Advanced Sensing and Signal Processing, including Wi-Fi Sensing and Brain-Computer Interfaces (EEG and fNIRS), as well as Robotics and UAV/Drone Systems. He co-founded and leads the Prometheus Lab (https://prometheus.di.uniroma1.it/) and co-founded the Sapienza startup 4AI (https://www.4aitech.it/), where he leads and supervises the Computer Vision team.
