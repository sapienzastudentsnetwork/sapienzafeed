03/04/2026 - NOTICE FOR FEMALE STUDENTS – SCHOLARSHIPS BY BIRRA PERONI S.R.L.

The Faculty of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Statistics of Sapienza University of Rome has issued a call for applications for the award of 12 scholarships, each worth €2,500 (gross of applicable taxes), funded by Birra Peroni S.r.l. as part of the BeHer initiative, aimed at promoting gender equality.

Eligibility
The scholarships are intended for outstanding female students enrolled in the academic year 2025/2026 in the Faculty of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Statistics, in the following degree programmes:

Bachelor’s degree programmes: Computer Science (1 scholarship); Computer Science – primarily distance learning (1 scholarship); Information Engineering (Latina campus) (1 scholarship); Computer and Control Engineering (2 scholarships); Electronic Engineering (2 scholarships).

Master’s degree programmes: Cybersecurity (1 scholarship); Engineering in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence – Computer Engineering (1 scholarship); Control Engineering (2 scholarships); Computer Science (1 scholarship); Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (1 scholarship).

Application procedure
Deadline for applications: May 2, 2026, at 11:59 PM.

Applications must be submitted exclusively via certified email (PEC) to:
presidenza-i3s@cert.uniroma1.it

using the institutional email account (@studenti.uniroma1.it).

Applications must be drafted on plain paper in accordance with the template attached to the call (Annex 1) and must include: Infostud student ID number; full name; institutional email address; tax identification code; phone number; degree programme in which the applicant is enrolled for the academic year 2025/2026; declarations required under Article 4 of the call; copy of a valid identity document.

All documentation must be submitted as a single PDF file with a maximum size of 2 MB.

Evaluation and ranking
Applications will be assessed by an Evaluation Committee, which will draw up a merit-based ranking based on the weighted average grade and the number of credits earned relative to the total required.

In the event of a tie, preference will be given to younger candidates.

The provisional ranking will be published on the University Transparency website:
https://trasparenza.uniroma1.it/page/5/bandi-di-concorso.html ↗

Requests for review may be submitted within 5 calendar days from publication via institutional email to:
presidenza-i3s@cert.uniroma1.it

Scholarship award
Selected candidates will be contacted via their institutional email to accept the scholarship and provide the necessary details for payment. The scholarship will be paid in a single instalment within 60 days from the submission of the required information.

Administrative contact person
Ms. Marcella Volpe
Email: marcella.volpe@uniroma1.it

For further details, please refer to the full call at:
https://trasparenza.uniroma1.it/page/5/bandi-di-concorso.html ↗

Publication date: April 2, 2026
Application deadline: May 2, 2026

02/10/2025 - Methods of Completion for the Complementary Educational Activity (AFC) for the Master’s Degree Program in Computer Science

Methods of Completion for the Complementary Educational Activity (AFC) for the Master’s Degree Program in Computer Science
Students have several options to earn the 6 ECTS credits required for an AFC:

  1. choose from the AFCs offered each year by the Degree Program, which include classroom lectures and individual work;
  2. carry out a structured activity proposed by a faculty member, which requires individual work by the student, but no classroom lectures;
  3. agree with a faculty member on a topic for individual work, related to a course taught by the professor or to their research activity;
  4. pass the exam of a course not included in the student's completion plan, which can then be recorded as an AFC;
  5. obtain validation from the Committee for the Recognition of Extracurricular Activities for an activity carried out outside the University, to be recognized as an AFC.

The Complementary Educational Activities offered for the academic year 2025/26 are as follows:
• Type 1 AFC – include classroom lectures and individual work:

o Instructors: Toni Mancini, Enrico Tronci
Title: AI for Design and Verification of Safety/Mission Critical Intelligent Systems
