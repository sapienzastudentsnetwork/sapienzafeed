Study Plan Information
After enrollment, all students must submit their Study Plan through the university platform “Percorsi Formativi / GOMP / INFOSTUD”.
Please note that:

  • exams not included in the approved Study Plan cannot be registered;
  • first-year students may only take exams included in the first-year curriculum before the Study Plan is approved;
  • the list of available exams always refers to the academic year of first enrollment;
  • students transferring from other programmes or universities will have their Study Plan initialized after credit recognition by the administrative office.

Study Plan submission windows

  • First-year students only: from December 20th to January 10th
  • All academic years:
    • from September 20th to October 15th
    • from February 25th to March 15th

Study Plan structure
The programme includes:

  • Required courses
  • Optional courses divided into Groups A, B, C and D
  • Free-choice courses
  • Other educational activities (AAF)
  • Final Master Thesis

The detailed list of courses, credits (ECTS), optional groups, and regulations is available in the official document at the following link https://drive.google.com/file/d/1w1vxBeD-Pj8lovnIBQorP5NwGZAbO-mT/view ↗
