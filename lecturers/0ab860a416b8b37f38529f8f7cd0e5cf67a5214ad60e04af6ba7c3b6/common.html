Before joining Sapienza University as Associate Professor, I was a Poul Due Jensen Associate Professor at Aalborg University in Denmark, a Lise Meitner Fellow at TU Wien in Austria and an Assistant Professor at IMT Lucca in Italy. I was awarded an MSc in mathematics and a PhD in computer science by the LMU Munich in 2010 and 2014, respectively.

My research focuses on formal methods in the area of mathematical modeling, with an emphasis on the efficient simulation and optimization by means of algorithmic model reduction. Models of interest comprise real-world chemical reaction networks, electric circuits, neural networks and quantum circuits.
