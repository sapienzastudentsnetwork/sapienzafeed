Luigi V. Mancini published more than 150 scientific papers in international conferences and journals, and received more than 9500 citations.
He served on the program committees of several international conferences, among which: ACM Conference on Computer and Communication Security, ACM Symposium on Access Control Models and Technology, European Symposium on Research in Computer Security, and Financial Cryptography and Data Security Conference. He has served as a partner leader in several national and international research projects, including the Gen4Olive project (EC Horizon 2020, Grant Agreement No. 101000427), the SUNFISH project (EC Horizon 2020, Grant Agreement No. 644666), and GAINS (Geopolitics Aware INternet Strategies, Ref. HOME/2013/CIPS/AG/4000005057) funded by the European Commission – Directorate-General for Home Affairs.
Formerly, he has been the Vice Dean of the Facoltà di Ingegneria dell'Informazione, Informatica e Statistica, and chaired several Master’s degree programmes in Cybersecurity at Sapienza University of Rome.
Luigi V. Mancini received the PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Newcastle upon Tyne, UK, in 1989.
