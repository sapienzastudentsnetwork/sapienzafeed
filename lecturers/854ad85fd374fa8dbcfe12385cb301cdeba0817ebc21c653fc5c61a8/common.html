RESEARCH RELATED ACTIVITY
1-ORGANIZATION OF CONFERENCES:
Since 2010, together with A. Vitolo, F. Leoni and I. Capuzzo Dolceia, I have created and organised a cycle of conferences, the idea being to gather a highly qualified group of researchers interested in the study of elliptic equations via the Maximum Principle. The conferences have proved to be a success and are becoming a reference in the field. The first was ”Positivity: a key to fully-nonlinear equations” in Vietri, May 2010. Followed by
”Mostly maximum Principle” in Rome, September 2012 and ”MMP” in Agropoli, September 2015, MMP Banff 2017, MMP Cortona 2022. These conferences have become a key moment to evaluate the situation in the field. In 2018, organization of a conference in Agropoli
In may 2023 organization of “Nonlinear partial differential equations: theory, numerics and applications” at Sapienza
