The path of excellence is an integrative pathway that consists of educational activities and those of the course of study in which the student is enrolled. At the same time as graduation, a student who has completed a path of excellence receives a certificate of the course completed, issued by the Chair of the Faculty of the student's choice in the manner prescribed for other types of certification, which is recorded on the student's career.

Along with this certification, the University grants the student an award equal to the fees paid in the last year of the course.

Admission requirements and how to carry out the activities are specified in the announcement.

When applying for admission to the pathway of excellence, the student must indicate the proposal(s) of preference among those indicated by the course and, at his/her discretion, he/she will receive an email regarding this - after having submitted an application to participate in the course.

The topics are published for each academic year.

The announcement is unique to faculty and has been published in Sapienza Transparent Administration.
