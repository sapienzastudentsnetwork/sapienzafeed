The announcement

Topics proposed by the department for academic year 24-25

A path of excellence is planned for the bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science. The path of excellence is an integrative path that consists of educational activities and those of the course of study in which the student is enrolled. At the same time as graduation, a student who has completed a path of excellence receives a certificate of the course completed, issued by the Chair of the faculty of affiliation in the manner provided for other types of certification, which is recorded on the student's career.

With this certification, the University gives the student an award equal to the fees paid in the last year of the course.

Just to let you know, admission requirements and the manner of carrying out the activities are specified in the announcement.

When applying for admission to the pathway of excellence, the student must indicate the proposal(s) of preference among those indicated by the department. The proposals are published for each academic year.

The announcement is unique to faculty and is published in Sapienza's transparent administration.
