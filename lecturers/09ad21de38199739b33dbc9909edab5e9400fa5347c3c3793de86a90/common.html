Exam modality: written and oral exam. The written exam will consist in 6 or 9 questions depending on the CFU class (9 CFU for Quantitative Economics, 6 CFU for the other curricula, Quantitative Finance, Data Analyst, Official Statistics and Data Science). The questions are exercises (solved during the course) and open analytical questions. The oral exam begins with the correction of the written exam.
