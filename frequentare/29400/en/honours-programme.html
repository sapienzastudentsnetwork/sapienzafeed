The announcement

Topics proposed by the department for academic year 24-25

The activation of a path of excellence for the bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science is planned.

The path of excellence is an integrative path that consists of training activities additional to those of the study course in which the student is enrolled. At the same time as obtaining the degree, the University awards the student a prize equal to the amount of fees paid in the last year of the course.

The access requirements and how to carry out the activities are specified in the announcement.

When submitting the application for admission to the excellence path, the interested person must indicate the proposal (or proposals) of preference among those indicated by the department. Proposals are published for each individual academic year.

The call is unique to each faculty and is published in Sapienza transparent administration.
